About Me

I’m a passionate software engineer with the persistence and creativity to solve any puzzle I encounter. I have experience developing dynamic full stack web applications in JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, HTML, and CSS.

I really enjoy being able to have an idea about an app and bringing it to life, it’s truly magical how much you can do with writing a few lines of code. Coding has allowed me to express my creativity and to develop apps that can have a positive impact on society.
